Hey everyone, welcome to my website. Or at least, the third iteration of one. And the simplest of them all technology-wise. Just a plain old HTML and CSS file. And a slightly long read if you're bothered. It's okay. With how much the modern adult's attention span has dwindled due to the dopamine-driven design of short-form video and social media, I'm sure one article is manageable 👍.

Now, perhaps it would only be proper if I begin with a self-introduction. I was born under the name Tianze (天泽), but I go by just Leo nowadays. I've always wondered why my parents chose my English name to be Leo. When I asked, the response was "You were named after a very famous star.". Leonardo DiCaprio maybe 🤔?

Let's talk about programming and software development. TypeScript and Python are my most used languages. I would consider myself to be a web frontend enthusiast. My favourite frontend framework is Vue.js and my most used component library is Nuxt UI. Though I think I want to create my own component library someday. Add a little more variety to the Vue ecosystem I suppose. Check out whatever scraps of work I have on my GitHub I guess. Though I use the pseudonym Robert Ling. It was a name a friend gave me.

Gaming next 🎮. I play Team Fortress 2 primarily. A timeless masterpiece, as many have called it, and indeed I think of it as one myself. From 2017 to now, it has been years since I've first booted up the game. How time flies. It's crazy to think that this game has accompanied me for almost an entire decade. To me, I still think 2007 was one decade ago. My favourite class to play is the Heavy. I like the Mann vs. Machine gamemode the most. Yes, it's basically gambling for golden-colored weapons - but hey, still cheaper per week than getting one lunch out in the Sydney CBD (and getting an "Australium" weapon sounds cool fight me).

Music 🎵. Let's talk about music. Nowadays, I find myself listening to Chinese songs a lot more than I do English songs. I guess that's one way of rediscovering part of your cultural heritage as a migrant. But another reason is that, if you take a moment to process the lyrics of many Mandopop/Cantopop songs, they are often much more artistic and "poetic" than English pop songs. My favourite band is Mayday (五月天) and my favourite song is Second Life (第二人生).

How about I share my experiences with life? Who wouldn't want to hear a random person on the internet tell their tale of how they roamed this planet we call Earth? Honestly, I sometimes think about the days I've been alive and think - what the hell am I even doing? A midlife crisis before I'm halfway through my 20s. But I guess, when you see people you thought you'd known for a long time drift away from you, old schoolmates breaking into prestigious companies, moving to Silicon Valley, getting into relationships, while I'm just still... here, anyone would feel left behind. But life moves on. If time can't stop for me, then I sure need to get a move on. No idea if that means anything, but at least it sounded like something 😎.
